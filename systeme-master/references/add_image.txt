How to add an image to a page
In this article, you will learn how to add an image to a page.
To get started, you will need:
A
systeme.io
account
A page in edit mode
Adding an image
In the page editor, drag and drop the
Image
element (1) from the panel on the left onto your page.
Click the settings icon (2), then click the upload icon (3) to open the file selection popup.
Select the
Upload
tab (4) to add a new image from your computer, or choose the
Your images
tab (5) to use a previously uploaded one.
You can also check the box to
Optimize uploaded images
(6). This automatically compresses your files to improve page loading speed, with no noticeable loss in quality.
Add the image to your page by clicking
Insert
(6).
Making the image clickable
Click on the image to display its settings in the panel on the left. Select
Open URL
from the
Action when image clicked
dropdown (7), then paste your URL into the
Link
field (8).
