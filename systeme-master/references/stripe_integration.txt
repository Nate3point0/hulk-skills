How to connect your Stripe account to systeme.io
In this article, you will learn how to connect your Stripe account to
systeme.io
.
To get started, you will need:
A
systeme.io
account
A Stripe account (
click here to create a Stripe account
)
To connect your systeme.io account to your Stripe account, click on your profile picture and then
Settings
(1). Next, in the menu on the left side of your page, click
Payment Gateways
(2).
On the payment gateway options page, click on
Connect
(3) for the Stripe option.
You will be redirected to a page where you can log in to your Stripe account or create a new one.
First, select the country where your business is registered, and the type of business (individual, company, or nonprofit organization).
On the next screen, verify your personal information. This is required to activate payments on systeme.io.
Once completed, click Authorize access to this account. You will be redirected back to systeme.io.
Note:
For Stripe to work correctly, the Email and First Name fields are required on your payment pages.
