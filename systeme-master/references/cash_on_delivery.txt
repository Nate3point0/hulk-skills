How to set up cash on delivery payment on systeme.io
In this article, you will learn how to provide "cash on delivery" as an option to your customers on your payment pages.
To offer this option, you need to connect the corresponding payment gateway.
Configuring the payment gateway
From your systeme.io account, click on your profile picture and select
Settings
(1), then go to
Payment gateways
(2).
In the list of gateways, find
Cash on delivery
and click
Connect
(3).
If needed, add any customer instructions (4), then click on
Save
(5).
Note:
It may take some time for the integration to be completed.
Activating "Cash on delivery" for your funnel
In the funnel editor, click on
Funnel settings
(6). On the
Edit funnel
popup that appears, click the
Cash on delivery
payment checkbox (7). Finally, click on
Save
to confirm your changes (8).
After following these steps, customers will now see C.O.D. as a payment option on your sales funnel payment pages.
Editing the instructions or deleting the integration
You can edit the instructions by clicking on
Edit customer instructions
(9), or delete the integration by clicking the
trash icon
(10).
Note:
Cash on Delivery is only available for physical products.
