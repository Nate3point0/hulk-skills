How to link a sales funnel or a page to your external website
This article shows you how to link a systeme.io funnel or page to an external website (e.g., WordPress).
What you will need:
A
systeme.io
account
An active sales funnel (learn how to create a new funnel
here
)
1. On your external website, create a link or button that points to a custom URL.
2. In systeme.io, copy the URL of your funnel or page:
Click
Funnels (1)
in the top menu, then select the target funnel (2).
Select the first step of the funnel and click the
copy icon (3)
next to the URL path.
To link to a specific step inside the funnel (e.g., the order form), select that step and
copy its URL (4)
3. Return to your external website. Open the settings for your link or button and paste the URL you just copied.
Note:
To embed a form or popup directly onto your website, please read
this article
.
