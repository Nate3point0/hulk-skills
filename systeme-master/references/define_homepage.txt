How to define your domain's homepage
In this article, you will learn how to point your domain name's homepage to a blog, a website, a creator store, or a sales funnel page created with
systeme.io.
What you will need:
A
systeme.io
account
A blog, a website, a sales funnel, or a creator store
A domain name
1) For the blog:
Go to the
Sites
tab on your menu, click on
Blogs
(1).
Click on the 3 dots, then on
Settings
(2).
Delete the text from the URL path field (leave the field empty) and click on
Save
(3).
2) For a sales funnel page:
Go to the sales funnel page to set as your homepage and delete the text from the URL path field (leave the field empty).
After updating the path, it will be saved automatically.
3) For a website:
Go to the
Sites
tab on your menu, click on
Websites
(1).
Click on the 3 dots, then on
Settings
(2).
Delete the text from the URL path field (leave the field empty) and click on
Save
(3).
4) For a creator store:
Go to the
Sites
tab on your menu, click on
Creator stores
(1).
Click on the 3 dots, then on
Settings
(2).
Delete the text from the URL path field (leave the field empty) and click on
Save
at the bottom of the page.
