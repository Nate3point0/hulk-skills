## Upgrade notes (what "good" looks like beyond the basics)
- Keep titles under ~60 characters, descriptions under ~155 — they truncate.
- Description = your hook + CTA, because it doubles as the social-share preview text.
- Keywords field is largely ignored by Google — fill lightly, never stuff.
- Bigger SEO wins than meta tags: custom domain on everything (motocity.autos), a real og-image per page (the image prompts cover this), and the hub interlinking all funnels (already done via the doors + footers).
