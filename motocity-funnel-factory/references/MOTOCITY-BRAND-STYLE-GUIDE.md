## LAYOUT SPECIFICATIONS

### Document Setup
- **Size:** US Letter (8.5" × 11")
- **Orientation:** Portrait
- **Margins:** 0.75" left/right, 0.6" top/bottom
- **Color Mode:** RGB (digital PDFs)

### Page Structure (7 Pages Standard)

**PAGE 1 — COVER**
- Background: `#0A0A0A` with subtle radial gradient (gold glow from center, 10% opacity)
- Logo: Top center, 180–200px width
- Title: Centered, Bebas Neue, 60–72pt, gold
- Subtitle: Centered, Montserrat, 18pt, silver
- Decorative line: 2px gold horizontal rule below subtitle
- Footer: "MOTOCITY.AUTOS" in small gold text, bottom center

**PAGE 2 — AUTHOR/BRAND PAGE**
- Background: `#1A1A1A`
- Logo: Top left, 80px width
- Headline: "About This Guide" or "From the Desk of NateFunkadelic"
- Body: 2–3 sentences about who you are and why this matters
- Contact block: Email + social handles in gold text
- Divider: Thin gold line

**PAGE 3 — HOW TO USE**
- Background: `#0A0A0A`
- Quick 3-step instruction on getting value from the guide
- Gold checkbox icons for any checklist items
- This page sets expectations

**PAGE 4–6 — CONTENT (The Meat)**
- Background: `#0A0A0A`
- Section headers: Gold left-border accent (4px vertical line)
- Body text: Silver on black
- Checklists: Gold empty squares (☐) for print/digital checkability
- Red flags/warnings: Red text with ⚠️ or 🚨 icon
- Pro tips: Gold background box with black text (or gold border box)
- Tables: Dark gray header row, alternating row backgrounds

**PAGE 7 — CTA / NEXT STEPS**
- Background: `#0A0A0A` with gold gradient overlay (subtle)
- Headline: "What's Next?" in gold
- Tripwire teaser: Brief pitch for the paid upsell
- Primary CTA button: Gold background, black text, full width
- Affiliate CTA (if applicable): Secondary button, outlined gold
- Footer: Logo, website, email, social icons

**PAGE 8 — BACK COVER (Optional)**
- Background: `#0A0A0A`
- Centered logo, large
- Tagline in gold
- All links in white/silver
- "Share this guide with someone who needs it"

## DESIGN ELEMENTS

### Icons & Symbols
- **Checkboxes:** Gold squares (☐ for empty, ☑ for filled) — use Canva shapes
- **Bullet points:** Gold circles (•) or chevrons (›)
- **Warning icons:** Red triangles or 🚨 emoji
- **Pro tips:** Gold lightbulb icon or 💡 emoji
- **External links:** Small arrow icon (↗) after linked text

### Divider Lines
- **Primary:** 2px solid gold, full width
- **Secondary:** 1px solid `#333333`, 60% width, centered
- **Section breaks:** Thin gold line with diamond icon in center

### Cards / Boxes
- **Pro Tip Box:** `#1A1A1A` background, 1px gold border, 12px padding, rounded corners (8px)
- **Warning Box:** `#1A1A1A` background, 1px red border, 12px padding
- **CTA Box:** Gold gradient background, `#0A0A0A` text, 16px padding, rounded corners

### Shadows & Depth
- **Text shadows:** Subtle drop shadow on headlines (2px down, 4px blur, 20% opacity black)
- **Card shadows:** 0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)
- **No heavy gradients:** Keep it clean and premium

## PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGE STYLE

- **Car detail photos:** High contrast, glossy reflections, dark backgrounds
- **DJ/event photos:** Stage lighting, dynamic angles, motion blur acceptable
- **Screenshots:** Dark mode interfaces preferred; add subtle gold border
- **Product shots:** Isolated on black or gradient background
- **No stock photos that look like stock photos:** Use your own imagery only

---

## VOICE & TONE (Content Style)

- **Direct.** No fluff. Military precision.
- **Actionable.** Every section ends with a "do this now" instruction.
- **Confident.** "This is what works." Not "this might help maybe."
- **Aggressive but helpful.** Like a coach, not a lecturer.
- **Revenue-focused.** Every tip ties back to saving money or making money.
- **No corporate speak.** "Use this" not "it is recommended that one utilizes."

## AFFILIATE LINK EMBEDDING RULES

### Primary CTA Placement (Per PDF)
1. **Page 2 (Author page):** Soft mention — "Built with Systeme.io" small link
2. **Page 4 (Mid-content):** Natural inline mention where relevant
3. **Page 7 (CTA page):** Full affiliate CTA button, prominent
4. **Page 8 (Back cover):** Small text link in footer

### Link Formatting
- **Primary affiliate CTA:** Gold button, black text, centered, full width
- **Inline links:** Gold text, underlined, followed by ↗
- **URL display:** Full URL shown in monospace, silver, 9pt — so people can type it if the hyperlink fails

## CANVA WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Create Master Template
1. Open Canva → Create a design → US Letter Document
2. Set up Page 1 (Cover) with all brand elements
3. Duplicate to create 8 pages
4. Save as "MotoCity Lead Magnet Template"

### Step 2: Apply Per Lead Magnet
1. Duplicate the master template
2. Rename to match the lead magnet (e.g., "Car Maintenance Checklist")
3. Replace title text
4. Copy-paste content from my markdown files, page by page
5. Apply the typography specs above
6. Add relevant photos/screenshots where noted
7. Export as PDF Print

### Step 3: Export Settings
- **File type:** PDF Print
- **Color profile:** RGB (for digital distribution)
- **Quality:** High
- **No crop marks or bleed** (unless printing physically)
