## Tips for Better Outputs

**flux_schnell (images):**
- Add `commercial photography, professional, 8k, high detail` to most prompts
- Specify lighting explicitly: `studio lighting`, `natural window light`, `dramatic side lighting`
- Add negative space intentionally: `with space on left for text overlay`
- For thumbnails: `designed for YouTube thumbnail, eye-catching, high contrast`

**TI2V (animation):**
- Keep motion descriptions simple — complex multi-step motions often fail
- Best motions: slow rotation, gentle breathing, subtle camera push/pull, drifting clouds
- Avoid: fast movement, multiple subjects moving, text animation, complex physics
- Short clips (17 frames) work better for tight loops
